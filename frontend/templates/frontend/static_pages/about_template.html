{% trans 'In Sport Nutrition LT' %}

{% blocktrans %} Sports Nutrition is a branch of Human Nutrition that specializes in guiding the diet of people who perform intense or moderate physical activities in a professionally or not. Depending on the determined goals of the sports routine that is done, attention will be focused on one or other nutrients, as well as on the amount and distribution of the same during the day. In addition these nutritional treatments take into account all the cycles of the sport: rest, the active phase and the recovery phase. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} An individualized nutritional plan, can optimize the performance of people in physical training, achieve better results in activity and achieve a faster recovery after the wear and tear they are subjected to. It also has the power to modify the body composition of the individual until reaching the proposed initial goal. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} In this way sports nutrition accompanied by genetic factors and the type of training according to the sport being carried out, is ONE of the three factors that mark the practice of sport. For this reason there can not be a generalized diet, each treatment must be adjusted to the individual and particular characteristics of each person (genetic load and lifestyle). {% endblocktrans %}
